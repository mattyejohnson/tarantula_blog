(Alright, their proper common name is Orange Baboon Tarantula, but Orange Bitey Thing is more accurate)

Four things about OBTs:
1. They can bite
2. They will bite
3. They love to bite
4. Their bites can sometimes put you in the hospital

Appealing? Maybe not. But if you're an experienced handler, consider this list as well:

1. Some OBTs do a little happy dance after they kill their prey. It's adorable

3. They are so angry all the time, but in an endearing way, y'know?
4. You can come up with fun reasons for their names that you can tell everyone that asks about them. Mine for the above tarantula is: His name is Hannibal, because he's medically significant (being eaten is somewhat medically significant, no?)
